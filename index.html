ReekieDram – Your Whisky Hop App

Discover, plan, and enjoy your perfect whisky adventure in Edinburgh

About the App

ReekieDram – Edinburgh Edition – is your personalized whisky journey planner. Designed for travelers and whisky lovers alike, the app helps you uncover top bars, unique distilleries, and trusted shops across the city — all based on your taste and travel time.

Key Scenarios

  • Discover by Taste: Choose what you like (e.g., peaty, sherry, smooth) and instantly get top whiskies — with bars that serve them.
  • 2–3 Day Whisky Hop Planner: Get curated routes for exploring Edinburgh’s best whisky stops — day by day.
  • Distillery Explorer: Find must-visit distilleries based on location, style, or tradition — with clear directions.

What Makes ReekieDram Unique?

Unlike whisky rating apps or global databases, ReekieDram is built for real-world use in Edinburgh. It guides you through curated experiences and tasting adventures — no account needed, no fuss, just whisky joy.

Features

  • Curated whisky journeys based on your preferences
  • Detailed info on top-rated bars, distilleries, and shops
  • Integrated maps for easy navigation
  • Save and share your favourite trails
  • Fast and lightweight — no login required

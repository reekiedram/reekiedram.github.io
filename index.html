About the App

Your Whisky Hop – Edinburgh Edition – helps whisky lovers explore the best bars, distilleries, and shops in Edinburgh. Plan personalized bar hopping journeys based on your taste and preferences.

Features

  • Personalized whisky bar hop planning
  • Explore whiskies, bars, and distilleries with detailed info
  • Save and share your journeys
  • Integrated maps and in-app website viewing
